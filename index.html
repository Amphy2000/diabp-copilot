Frequently Asked Questions

The bot loads 300 historical 1-minute candles and computes EMA-9, EMA-21, and RSI-14 in real time. It fires a trade on two types of signal: (1) a fresh EMA crossover — the highest-confidence entry — and (2) a trend continuation signal when two consecutive candles confirm an ongoing trend. This means it trades actively throughout the session, not just during rare crossover events.

A configurable Stop Loss stops the session if losses exceed your limit. A Target Profit locks in gains and stops automatically. The optional Martingale recovery doubles your stake after a loss (up to a set number of steps) so a single win recovers the sequence. During recovery, the bot tightens its entry filters to protect capital.

The bot is completely free to use — no subscriptions, no hidden fees. We earn a small turnover commission from Deriv on trades placed through our affiliate link. This keeps the service free forever and aligns our incentives directly with your success.

How it works:
Each trade uses your Stake. If the trade loses, the bot retries with a bigger stake (Stake × Multiplier) up to Max Recovery Steps times to recover the loss. When the Session Target is hit, the bot stops and locks your profit. When the Stop Loss is hit, the bot stops to protect you from further losses.

Rule of thumb: Stop Loss = 10× your stake. Target = 3× your Stop Loss.
