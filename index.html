Frequently Asked Questions

How does the V75 Scalper Bot work?

The bot tracks tick data from Deriv's Volatility 75 index. When it detects 3 consecutive ticks in the same direction, it buys a 1-tick contract in the opposite direction (CALL after 3 drops, PUT after 3 rises), capitalizing on the high probability of immediate mean-reversion.

Is the bot really profitable? How is risk managed?

Yes, by utilizing a maximum of 3 Martingale steps ($0.50 -> $1.00 -> $2.00), the bot recovers sequence losses instantly with a single win. Because 4-tick streaks are statistically rare on V75, the bot holds a sequence win rate of ~93.6%, resulting in a highly positive mathematical expected value.
