# DiaBP-Copilot: Investor & Clinic Pitch Deck

This pitch deck is designed to present DiaBP-Copilot to health facilities, pharmacies, government agencies, and potential donors.

## 📢 Promotional Copy (For Launch)

DiaBP-Copilot is Nigeria's first integrated AI-powered care coordinator for hypertension and diabetes. By bridging clinicians, community pharmacies, and patients through an effortless, zero-install WhatsApp bot and a premium web dashboard, DiaBP-Copilot automates vitals tracking, guarantees medication adherence via SafeMeds refills, and deploys computer-vision AI to screen for diabetic foot complications.

Elevate patient outcomes, minimize emergency admissions, and streamline facility operations—all under custom branding at https://diabpcopilot.com. Onboard your facility in under 60 seconds.

## 📊 Presentation Slides

### Slide 1: Cover Slide
- **Title:** DiaBP-Copilot
- **Subtitle:** Reimagining Chronic Disease Care in Nigeria & Beyond
- **URL:** https://diabpcopilot.com
- **Core Value:** Connecting Clinicians, Pharmacies, and Patients via WhatsApp & AI.

### Slide 2: The Silent Epidemic
- **Problem:** Hypertension and Diabetes are leading causes of stroke, heart attacks, and amputations in Nigeria.
- **Friction Points:**
  - Standard apps are too complicated for elderly/rural patients.
  - Medication refills are missed due to lack of coordination.
  - Clinicians have no access to continuous patient home data.

### Slide 3: The Connected Care Ecosystem
- **Seamless Coordination:** A single platform syncing three key user groups:
  1. **Patients:** Log daily readings and order refills on WhatsApp.
  2. **Clinicians:** Monitor triage priority lists and view patient histories.
  3. **Pharmacies:** Fulfill recurring refill orders automatically.

### Slide 4: Zero-Friction Patient Engagement
- **Leveraging WhatsApp:** No need to download native apps or learn new software.
- **Easy Steps:**
  - Send "join bet-sense" to +1 415 523 8886.
  - Log blood pressure, glucose, or upload a foot scan photo.
  - Automated prompts guide patients when they forget to track.

### Slide 5: The Clinician Dashboard
- **Features:**
  - **Triage Queue:** Flags critical patient readings instantly.
  - **Notice Board:** Coordinates clinical shifts and notice handovers.
  - **Staff Management:** Role-based dashboards for Admins (Doctors) and Staff.
  - **SMS Nudges:** Send vital check-in notifications directly to patients.

### Slide 6: SafeMeds Adherence Engine
- **How it works:**
  - The system tracks prescription duration and alerts the patient.
  - Patient approves refills via WhatsApp menu option "2".
  - Automated routing to the patient's selected local community pharmacy.
- **Outcome:** Double-digit improvements in patient drug adherence.

### Slide 7: AI Diabetic Foot Screening
- **Preventing Amputations:**
  - Early-stage diabetic neuropathy and ulcer hotspots are invisible to the eye.
  - Patients snap foot photos on WhatsApp; computer-vision models assess risk.
  - Clinicians are immediately alerted to schedule active podiatry consults.

### Slide 8: Revenue Split & Sustainability
- **Direct Payouts:**
  - Integrated Paystack payouts route patient subscription/drug fees.
  - **95% to the Facility/Pharmacy** instantly.
  - **5% Platform Commission** covers hosting, AI API costs, and SMS bundles.

### Slide 9: Installable PWA & Offline Support
- **Reliable Operation:**
  - Installable on home screen directly from Chrome.
  - Offline-first cache ensures staff can access records without active internet.
  - Auto-syncs changes immediately upon reconnecting.
